About

Erin Radtke was born in Indianapolis and graduated from Bishop Chatard High School in 2005. She double majored in Studio Art and Art History at Hanover College, maintaining a focus in photography within both majors. She graduated with the top honor in both of her majors, winning awards for Excellence in both Studio Art and Art History.

Since her youth, Erin has had a passion and talent for art and later discovered how to blend it into photography and portraiture. She believes that her photography is successful because she has been able to use her aesthetic, creative, and compositional knowledge provided by her fine art education within her images.

Being young and passionate about her work, she hopes to provide a service for others that will allow them to feel comfortable and natural. With this, she also provides her clients with an edginess and sense of control that she wishes she had had with photographers.
